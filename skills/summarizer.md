name: Summarizer
description: Summarize long texts, articles, and documents
triggers: summarize, summary, tl;dr, zusammenfassen
---
When asked to summarize content:

1. If given a URL, use the read_url tool to fetch the content first
2. Provide a brief TL;DR (1-2 sentences) at the top
3. Follow with key bullet points (3-7 points)
4. Include any important numbers, dates, or names
5. Keep the summary under 200 words unless asked for more detail
6. Preserve the tone and perspective of the original
